Frequently Asked Questions

Is the generated content truly unique?

Yes. Our AI generates content from scratch based on your prompts. It is designed to be plagiarism-free, but we always recommend running it through a plagiarism checker for academic or professional work.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Absolutely. Any content you generate is yours to use as you wish, including for commercial projects like marketing, ad copy, and monetized blogs.

What languages do you support?

Currently, our primary language is English. However, the AI has a strong understanding of many other languages and can often generate high-quality content in Spanish, French, German, and more. We are actively working on official multi-language support.

How does Serphi AI handle data privacy?

We take your privacy seriously. Your prompts and generated content are not used to train our models, and your conversation history is stored only in your browser's local storage for your convenience. It is not transmitted to our servers outside of the API call itself.
